## What data we access

Sheet Health Check reads the formulas and display values in the spreadsheet where it is installed. This access is used solely to scan for errors, inconsistencies, and performance issues.

## What data we store

**None.** Sheet Health Check does not store, save, transmit, or share any of your spreadsheet data. All scanning happens locally within Google's infrastructure. No data leaves Google's servers.

## What data we send to external servers

**Nothing.** Sheet Health Check makes zero external network calls. It does not use any third-party APIs, analytics services, or tracking tools.

## Permissions

Sheet Health Check requests access only to the specific spreadsheet where it is installed (`spreadsheets.currentonly` scope). It cannot access any other files in your Google Drive or Google Account.

## Google API Services User Data Policy

Sheet Health Check's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.
