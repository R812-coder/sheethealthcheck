Sheet Health Check

Spell check, but for your spreadsheet formulas.

Sheet Health Check is a Google Sheets add-on that scans your entire spreadsheet in seconds and finds problems you'd never catch manually.

What it checks

  • Formula errors — finds every #REF!, #DIV/0!, #N/A, #VALUE!, and more across all sheets
  • Inconsistent formulas — detects when a column of formulas has one cell that doesn't match the pattern
  • Hardcoded values — flags cells where someone accidentally pasted a value over a formula
  • Empty formula results — finds formulas that return blank, indicating possible broken logic
  • Performance issues — identifies excess empty rows and columns slowing down your spreadsheet

How it works

Open Sheet Health Check from the menu, click "Run Health Check," and get your health score with a categorized list of every issue. Click any issue to jump directly to the problem cell.

Fast and private

Sheet Health Check runs entirely within Google's infrastructure. Your data never leaves Google's servers. No external APIs, no tracking, no data collection.
